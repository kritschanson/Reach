Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Overview

Reach is a browser-based tool that helps educators analyze student assessment data. We are committed to protecting the privacy of students, teachers, and facilitators who use Reach. This policy explains what data Reach handles, where it is stored, and how it is used.

Data We Do Not Collect

Reach does not collect, transmit, store, or have access to any student data. The application runs entirely within your browser. No student names, scores, identifiers, or assessment results are ever sent to Reach's servers — because Reach has no servers.

How Your Data Is Stored

When you sign in with Google, Reach uses the Google Drive API to save your analytics files, team settings, and column mappings to a folder called "Reach Analytics" in your own Google Drive account. This folder is visible to you and only accessible by Reach. You can view, manage, or delete these files directly from Google Drive at any time. You can revoke Reach's access to your Google Drive at any time from your Google Account settings.

Google Sign-In

Reach uses Google OAuth 2.0 for authentication. When you sign in, Reach receives your name, email address, and profile picture from Google, which are displayed within the app during your session. Reach does not store this information beyond your active session. We do not share your Google account information with any third party.

Google Drive Access

Reach requests the drive.file scope, which grants access only to files that Reach creates. Reach cannot read, modify, or delete any other files in your Google Drive. All Drive access is used solely to save and load your Reach Analytics folder and its contents.

Student Data and FERPA

Because student data is processed entirely within your browser and stored only in your own Google Drive account, Reach does not function as a "school official" or "third-party service provider" under FERPA. Your institution retains full control of student data at all times. We recommend reviewing your district's policies on the use of Google Drive for storing student information.

Data Security

All data transmitted between your browser and Google Drive is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS. Reach does not introduce any additional data transmission. The security of your data within Google Drive is governed by Google's security practices and your own Google account security settings.

Children's Privacy

Reach is designed for use by educators and facilitators, not directly by students. Reach does not knowingly collect any personal information from individuals under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of Reach after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at reachplcapp@gmail.com.
